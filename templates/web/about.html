About Us

KMT Silks, the largest clothing showroom in Malappuram, has sunk its roots into the hearts of the customers so deep that it is one of the most trusted brands in Kerala today. Our very first showroom was opened in Perinthalmanna in 1996 which continues its quality with dignity even after three glorious decades of dedicated service. Be it in the case of products, services or shopping experience, KMT Silks has always been inclined to provide the customers with the best in all kinds. We push our boundaries so as to offer you limitless possibilities. Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted and beloved brand that sets the standard for modern retail fashion. We aim to build a clothing shop that serves as an inclusive space where style meets comfort and where our customers feel at home.

Mission

At Kmt Silks our mission is to provide an exceptional shopping experience that blends style, comfort, and affordability. We are committed to offering a diverse range of clothing that caters to various tastes, sizes, and preferences, ensuring that every customer finds pieces that reflect their individuality. By sourcing high-quality materials and staying on top of the latest fashion trends, we aim to create a collection that not only makes our customers look good but also feel confident in their choices. We strive to make fashion accessible for all, offering both timeless classics and contemporary pieces that are as versatile as the people who wear them. Our dedication goes beyond just selling clothes; we believe in fostering a community where everyone feels welcome, valued, and empowered.
